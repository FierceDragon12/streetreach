About StreetReach

StreetReach is a lean, student-run flyer distribution company focused on one goal: helping small businesses in Acton get seen. We operate with speed, precision, and local knowledge — powered by high school students who care about real results.

Flyer marketing may seem old-school, but when it's targeted and personal, it performs. According to a 2023 DMA report, direct mail gets a 4.4% response rate, outperforming both email and paid search. A Royal Mail study found 89% of people remember receiving a flyer — and nearly half keep it.

In June 2025, we ran a 3-week trial in Bangalore with 3 local clients. Each paid ₹6,000–₹9,000 (~$70–$105) for targeted flyer drops in high-traffic areas. The campaign generated over ₹25,000 (~$300) in total revenue, boosting local awareness and foot traffic for every client.
